30 Minutes Film Festival invites videographers and directors around the world to share their work produced-planned, filmed, and editted-in thirty minutes.

The festival ignites instant creativity within artists. Participants experience a different aspect of creativity by producing a piece in such a short span of time.

Artists from diverse cultural and ethnical backgrounds participate in the 30 Minutes Film festival every year.

In 2022, more than fifteen artists took part in the festival and shared a wide variety of films ranging from milkshake advertisement to fashion show archive. Other productions will be shared shortly in each artist's website.
